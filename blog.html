justify-content: center

justify-content: center; is broken. When the screen gets too small, items inside can overflow the left/right edges. However, the scrollbar will only let you scroll to the right. Anything on the left side is inaccessible.

A better approach is to use justify-content: left; on your flexbox and margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; on the offending child. This way, overflows will only go past the right edge and will be scrollable.
